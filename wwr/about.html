Big Rotund Floating Down The River Rafting Company

Here at the Big Floating Rotund Down the River Rafting Company, our motto is "If Its Big, Round, and Floats, We'll Float It Down A River!

History

Founded in 1916, we got our start in France, where Lord Archibald Penniesnoutworth IV from England made the daring voyage down the Somme on a makeshift barge constructed out of barrels, loose boards, and extra lifeboats from the RMS Titanic.

After returning to England early after a German artillery shell sunk his raft and killed half his party, Lord Archibald Penniesnoutworth IV moved the the States and brought his new rafting ideas to the New World and now we send anything thats round down any big river!
