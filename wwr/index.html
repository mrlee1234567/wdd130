About This Site

Welcome to the homepage of the Big Rotund Floating Down The River Rafting Company! Here at BRFDTRC, we send big and round floating objects down the rivers of the world! From the Colorado to the Ganges, if we can send things down it, well send it!

Here at our homepage, you can contact us to get in touch for a trip down river. We offer everything from rentals to trips to life insurance policies to training! Simply navigate to the trips page to book with us, or if you have other questions, check out our contact page.
